# MLSecOps Field Manual™ - Brand Guidelines

## Logo

**Primary Logo:** `mlsecops-fm-logo-master.png`  
**Resolution:** 2080 x 2048 px  
**Format:** PNG with transparency-ready background

### Logo Elements

- **Surveillance Eye:** Central motif representing ML model monitoring and security oversight
- **Neural Network Nodes:** Interconnected nodes radiating from eye = ML/AI systems under observation
- **Scan Lines:** Horizontal lines across eye = active monitoring/detection
- **Circular Badge:** Military unit insignia aesthetic for field manual credibility
- **Star Accent:** Top center, reinforcing military/tactical heritage

### Text Elements

| Element | Placement | Style |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| MLSECOPS | Top arc | Bold, condensed, white |
| FIELD MANUAL | Bottom | Bold, condensed, white |
| VIGILIS IN MACHINA | Below Field Manual | Italic, smaller, muted red |

**Motto Translation:** "Watchful in the Machine"

## Typography

### Recommended Fonts

**Headers/Display:**
- Primary: **Arial Black** or **Impact** (matches logo aesthetic)
- Alternative: **Oswald**, **Bebas Neue**, **Anton**

**Body Text:**
- Primary: **Inter**, **Source Sans Pro**
- Monospace (code): **JetBrains Mono**, **Fira Code**

**Motto/Taglines:**
- **Times New Roman** or **Georgia** (serif, italic)

---

## Logo Usage

### Minimum Size
- Digital: 64px minimum width
- Print: 0.75" minimum width

### Clear Space
Maintain padding equal to the height of the "M" in MLSECOPS on all sides.

### Do Not
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Change the colors outside the brand palette
- Add drop shadows or effects
- Place on busy backgrounds without contrast
- Rotate the logo

## Brand Voice

**Key Attributes:**
- Direct and actionable
- Security-conscious
- Practitioner-focused (not academic)
- Real-world, battle-tested approaches
